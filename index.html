FACILITIES

  • LIBRARY

    EU has an open shelved air-conditioned modern library system enriched with text and reference books as well as journals required by the students and faculties for study and research activities. The central library is spacious enough, computerized and connected with internet.

  • LABORATORIES

    SEU has different laboratories enriched with modern facilities for conducting practical classes. These are Computer Laboratory, ICE Laboratory etc. Besides, the University has common computer laboratories for computer practice and free internet browsing for all students.

  • MULTIMEDIA & OVERHEAD PROJECTOR SUPPORT

    Southeast University uses multimedia and overhead projectors in classes to facilitate understanding of the topics. Multimedia projectors are freely available to students for regular class presentations, workshops, seminars etc.

  • STUDENT COUNSELING SYSTEM

    A student of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering can closely interact with the Coordinator. He/she can discuss any matter with them, be it personal or academic. The Dean, SSE, is also available full time to meet with students and parents. Parents are most welcome to consult with the Dean and relevant Program Directors about any problems regarding their ward(s).
